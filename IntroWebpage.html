One historical figure you wish to be like?

I would wish to be like Harald Hardrada. Harald Hardrada, a Norwegian prince exiled at 15, embarked on a life of epic adventure. He first sought refuge in Kievan Rus', where he honed his skills as a warrior and earned the favor of powerful rulers. From there, he journeyed to Byzantium, becoming a legendary commander in the Varangian Guard, fighting across the empire and amassing great wealth. Returning to Norway, Harald seized the throne, but his thirst for adventure led him to launch a daring invasion of England in 1066. His saga ended in a fierce battle at Stamford Bridge, where the last great Viking fell, marking the end of the Viking Age.
